About Me

Currently, I am working as a lecturer at Macao Polytechnic University.

I obtained the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Chan-Tong Lam and Prof. Benjamin K. Ng at Macao Polytechnic University in 2023.

Before that, I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Lanzhou University of Technology in 2012 and City University of Hong Kong in 2013, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in physical layer wireless communications, particularly in

  • Cell-Free Massive MIMO;

  • Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces/Intelligent Reflecting Surfaces;

  • Near-Field Communications; and

  • Hardware Degradations in Wireless Communication Systems.

Reproducibility

  • I highly respect research reproducibility, thus the simulation codes of my published papers are elaborately provided. Check here or my GitHub for more details.

  • Additionally, I am also in charge of a 1.2k+ stars project in GitHub that aims to gather simulation codes related to RIS studies. Check here for more details.
