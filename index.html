About Me

I obtained the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Chan-Tong Lam and Prof. Benjamin K. Ng at Macao Polytechnic University in 2023.

I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Lanzhou University of Technology in 2012 and City University of Hong Kong in 2013, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in physical layer wireless communications, particularly in

  • Cell-Free Massive MIMO;

  • Reconfigurable Intelligent Surface/Intelligent Reflecting Surface; and

  • Practical Hardware Degradation in Wireless Communication Systems.
